Saman Bank DataCenter Migration Project

Data Center migrations are high profile projects because they are risky. Data center managers and their teams are always Worried about lost data and excessive downtime. Migrating your data center is a complex process that requires a high degree of coordination with little tolerance for failure. The goals of any move are to:

  • Minimize or eliminate downtime during the move
  • Maintain or enhance the performance, disaster recovery and business continuity support of the data center

Scale of the project:

In data center we had more than 500 physical and Virtual servers and 5 storage systems and 3 superdome and many other devices. Project duration was about one and half year.


Upgrading VMware Vsphere 4.1 to VMware Vsphere Latest Version

VSphere is a sophisticated product with multiple components to upgrade.

In datacenter we had more than 200 important servers. many critical services are running on servers, So we had to upgrade VSphere version without downtime. The duration of upgrading was about 6 months.
